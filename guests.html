Guests & Speakers

We are honoured to welcome distinguished guests, cultural personalities, and scholars to grace Utkal Divas 2026 at NISER Bhubaneswar.

Invited Personalities

Distinguished personalities from diverse fields including social leadership, technology, literature & youth innovation

Sujit Mahapatra
Founder, Bakul Foundation
Social Entrepreneurship
A visionary social entrepreneur dedicated to fostering a culture of reading, storytelling, and volunteerism across Odisha. Through Bakul Foundation, he has championed grassroots-level initiatives promoting literacy and community engagement.
J. P. Jagadev
MD & CEO, Parameter Softwares (P) Ltd.
Technology & Heritage
A technology leader and cultural visionary bridging the gap between innovation and heritage. As Chairman of Udaygiri Foundation, he drives technology-enabled governance solutions while nurturing cultural preservation initiatives in Odisha.
Jatindra Kumar Nayak
Writer, Translator & Academic
Odia Literary Arts
A venerated figure in Odia literature known for his profound contributions to creative writing, literary translation, and academic scholarship. His works have enriched Odia cultural discourse and inspired generations of readers and writers.
Swetak Abhisek Mohapatra
Young Innovator & Thought Leader
Youth & Innovation
An emerging voice representing the aspirations and ideas of contemporary youth. His perspectives on innovation, technology, and social impact bring a fresh and dynamic dimension to conversations about Odisha's future.

✨ Stand-up Comedy Performance ✨

Debarchan Mishra

The evening comes alive with an electrifying stand-up comedy performance by Debarchan Mishra, a celebrated comedian bringing humor, wit, and contemporary perspectives. A perfect blend of entertainment and engagement that will leave the audience in splits!
