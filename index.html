About Utkal Divas

Utkal Divas, or Odisha Day, is celebrated every year on April 1st to commemorate the formation of Odisha as a separate province in 1936 — the first state in independent India to be carved out on a linguistic basis.

At NISER, Utkal Divas is a celebration of identity, culture, and pride - a day when the campus comes alive with poetry, art, music, dance, and knowledge. It is a tribute to the rich heritage of Odisha and the remarkable people who call this land home.

Join us on April 1, 2026 as we mark this special occasion with events that honour the traditions, arts, and intellectual spirit of Utkala Bharata.
